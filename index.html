Quick presentation of the project

Our project, FarSight, is an AI project (Machine Learning system) for a card game, applied to the game HearthStone (created by the Blizzard company). HearthStone is a digital collectible card game (and free-to-play) that revolves around turn-based matches between two opponents. The artificial intelligence has the ultimate goal of learning decks (list of cards played by a human), and be able to advice the player on what is the best decisions to take.

Our project is primarily for new or casual players from Hearthstone, giving them information about the opponent deck (and thus, his strategies, tips to play against, etc.). FarSight predicts the enemy deck, trying to predict what cards the opponent think play, or even learning the deck at the end of the game. The program is able to learn new cards and new strategies by itself. Its predictions and advice are improved based on the most currently play cards.
